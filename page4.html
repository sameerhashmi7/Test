Why I Have Interest In Coding:

Living in pakistan it seemed like an impossible thing to do. So, after completing my Intermediate studies i decided to join a university and pursue my interest in softwares, but everywhere i asked everyone said going to university is a waste of time in pakistan you should join some private institute to learn about Development. So i decided to join a institute and after some search i have found a couple of institutes, in which one of them was from kips college and kips college was a trusted brand. so i joined this institute called Global Age Trainings (by KIPS College). Right now i am learning Full-Stack Development and so far its going good, i have learned Git&Github,Vscode and HTML.

So these are my interests because of which i am here, lets hope one day i become a great programmer or a Develepor as i have always dreamed.
